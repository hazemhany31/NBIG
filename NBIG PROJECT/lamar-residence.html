About Lamar Residence

Lamar Residence is a gated residential community that combines elegant architecture, modern smart home features, and tranquil green landscapes. Designed for families seeking comfort, privacy, and a strong sense of community, Lamar Residence offers a haven away from the city's hustle, yet remains close to all the action in Egypt’s New Capital.

Enjoy 24/7 security, underground parking, and a prime location next to key city attractions. Experience a new standard of living in a vibrant and sustainable environment.

Modern Design & Innovation

Lamar Residence stands out with its contemporary facades, innovative layouts, and abundant natural light. Every apartment is designed for functionality and style, with smart systems for comfort and energy efficiency.

Green spaces and wide walkways are integrated throughout the compound, creating a serene, healthy environment for residents of all ages.
