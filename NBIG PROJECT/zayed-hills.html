About Zayed Hills

Zayed Hills is an exclusive residential compound in Sheikh Zayed City, offering a blend of modern architecture, smart layouts, and lush green spaces. Designed for families seeking luxury, privacy, and a vibrant community, Zayed Hills redefines contemporary living in West Cairo.

Enjoy 24/7 security, underground parking, and a prime location close to schools, shopping, and major city attractions—all in a sustainable, healthy environment.

Modern Design & Nature

The architecture of Zayed Hills draws inspiration from nature and modern minimalism. Homes feature open spaces, large windows, and smart home systems for comfort and energy efficiency.

Landscaped gardens and walkable pathways create a peaceful atmosphere and encourage a healthy lifestyle for all residents.

Amenities

Zayed Hills is designed for a modern family lifestyle, offering:

  • Central Park & Green Areas
  • Family Clubhouse
  • Children’s Playground
  • Jogging & Cycling Tracks
  • Fitness Center
  • Retail Promenade
  • 24/7 Security
  • Underground Parking
